How a Monolith is made

Four stages. One structure.

I.

Engineered concrete foundation

Soil analysis, engineered slab and pour — the base every Monolith stands on for the next hundred years.

II.

Steel-reinforced structure

Concrete columns and slabs with steel rebar, floor by floor. No shortcuts inside the walls.

III.

Stone & stucco craftsmanship

Grey stone, white stucco and warm wood detailing — the finish that gives each residence its face.

IV.

Landscape & handover

Lawns, hedges and pathways set the house into its lot. Then we hand you the keys — and stand behind the build.
